Four Cheese Pasta

    Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 250g Penne Pasta
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup shredded provolone cheese
  • ½ cup grated Romano cheese
