Virgin Mojito

    Ingredients (Serves 1)

  • Fresh lime juice
  • Honey Simple Syrup
  • Fresh mint leaves
    (or, use Mint Simple Syrup in a pinch)
  • Sparkling water
  • Crushed Ice cubes

Recipe

1. Place mint leaves and lime juice in a glass and muddle them together.

2. Crushing the leaves to release the flavor and oils the mint. Use a muddler or the handle of a wooden spoon.

3. Add the honey simple syrup.

4. Add the sparkling water. Garnish with lime slices, a sprig of mint, and/or fresh fruit
