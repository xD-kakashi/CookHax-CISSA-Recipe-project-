Iced Frappé

    Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 2 double shots of cold espresso
  • ¼ cup of whole milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 ounces ice cubes (about 1 cup)
  • Whipped cream for serving (optional)

Recipe

1. Place 2 double shots cold espresso, or 1/2 cup cold prepared instant espresso or strong brewed coffee in a blender.

2. Add the quarter cup of milk, vanilla extract and sugar to taste.

3. Lastly add the ice cubes in the blender. Begin on low speed and increasing to high speed, blend until thick, smooth, and creamy, 20 to 40 seconds.

4. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream if desired. Serve chilled.
